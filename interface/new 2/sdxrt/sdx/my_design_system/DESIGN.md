# Design System

This document outlines the core visual principles and components of our design system.

## Color Palette

Our brand utilizes a vibrant and distinct color palette to create a memorable user experience.

- **Primary Color:** `#651676` - Our dominant brand color, used for key actions and branding.
- **Secondary Color:** `#a181b3` - A supporting color that complements the primary hue, suitable for secondary actions and less prominent elements.
- **Tertiary Color:** `#df6c08` - An accent color used for highlights, alerts, and decorative elements to draw attention.
- **Neutral Color:** `#50815f` - A foundational color for backgrounds, text, and non-interactive UI elements.

## Typography

We employ a consistent typography system to ensure readability and hierarchy across all interfaces.

- **Headlines:** `inter` - Bold and clear, used for titles and prominent text.
- **Body Text:** `inter` - Highly readable for paragraphs and general content.
- **Labels:** `inter` - Concise and legible for form fields and interactive labels.

## Shape & Form

The visual language of our components is defined by their corner treatments and spacing.

- **Roundedness:** `Moderate (2)` - Our UI elements feature a moderate level of roundedness, providing a balanced and approachable aesthetic.
- **Spacing:** `Normal (2)` - A normal level of whitespace ensures a clean layout and comfortable user experience.

## Theme Details

- **Color Mode:** `light` - The system is primarily designed for a light interface, ensuring optimal contrast and readability in brightly lit environments.
